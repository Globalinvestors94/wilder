About Wilder Loans & Investment

Creating an environment for the rich and poor

Welcome to Wilder Loans & Investment, your trusted partner in financial growth and stability. At Wilder Loans & Investment, we specialize in providing tailored grants solutions and investment opportunities that empower individuals and businesses to achieve their financial goals.

Agricultural Loan

Agriculture remains the backbone of our economy, providing food, employment, and income for millions. However, many farmers face challenges such as limited access to resources, outdated farming techniques, and the impacts of climate change. The Sustainable Agricultural Development Initiative aims to address these issues by providing grants to support innovative, sustainable farming practices.

Business Loan

Small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) are crucial to economic development, job creation, and innovation. However, many businesses face significant barriers to growth, such as limited access to capital, technology, and expertise. The Innovative Business Growth and Sustainability Program aims to address these challenges by providing grants to support the expansion and sustainability of SMEs.

Housing/Assest Loan

Access to affordable housing and essential community assets is vital for the well-being and economic stability of individuals and families. However, many communities face a shortage of affordable housing and lack critical infrastructure and resources. The Affordable Housing and Community Assets Development Program aims to address these issues by providing grants to support the development of affordable housing and community assets.
